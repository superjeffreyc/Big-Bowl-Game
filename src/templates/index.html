Welcome to Big Bowl

A party game consisting of three rounds using the same word bank.



Enter the room code



How to play (At least 4 players required)

  • Divide the players into two groups.
  • Then, each player should individually submit words to the word bank. Do not share words with your team or opponents! (Recommendation is 5 words per player).
  • Once the game starts, each team takes turns to have one of its team members try to get team members to guess the current word on the screen.
  • The opposite team cannot guess the word. However, they should listen so they know what words have been used in the word bank.
  • Your team has 1 minute to guess as many words as possible before the timer runs out.
  • Once the word bank is empty, the game moves on to the next round.
  • The team with the most points after the three rounds wins!

The three rounds

  • ROUND 1: TABOO. Use any phrases or sentences to describe the word on the screen (except the word itself).
  • ROUND 2: CHARADES. Use any gestures or motions to describe the word on the screen (no talking!).
  • ROUND 3: PASSWORD. You can only say ONE word to describe the word on the screen (except the word itself).

How to get started with this app

  • Create a new room.
  • Optionally, you can have other people join your room and enter words. However, you can take turns using this device to input words.
  • Once you are satisfied with the size of the word bank, click Start Game.
  • Follow the instructions on the phone and begin playing the first turn!A party game consisting of three rounds using the same word bank.
  • When your turn is complete, pass this device to the opposite team and have their first player go next.
